Our Story

Welcome to Guwahati Electrical Complaint Service, your trusted partner for all electrical and appliance solutions in Assam. With an impressive 27 years of experience, we have been proudly serving Kamrup (Metro and Rural areas) with dedication and quick response times.

What started as a small electrical service has grown into a comprehensive solution provider, handling everything from simple repairs to complex installations, AC servicing, appliance repairs, modern EV charging installations, and even tender-based projects including supply of ACs, LT line installations, and electrical equipment.

We are now expanding our services to Nalbari, bringing our commitment to affordable rates, transparent pricing with no hidden charges, and quick response times to more communities. Our expansion has been earned through the trust of thousands of satisfied customers who have experienced our exceptional service.
